## The digital gap in UAE manufacturing

Most manufacturing and industrial companies in the UAE have invested heavily in plant, machinery, and certifications — but their marketing materials haven't kept pace. Outdated websites with PDF-only product listings, inconsistent branding across business cards and trade show banners, and zero search visibility mean these companies rely almost entirely on existing relationships and word of mouth. That works until a competitor with a better digital presence starts capturing the RFQs you never see.

## A marketing approach built for B2B industrial sales

We don't apply consumer marketing tactics to industrial companies. Instead, we focus on the touchpoints that matter in B2B procurement: a website that functions as a searchable product catalogue with clear specifications, technical content that demonstrates application knowledge, and SEO that targets the specific product and material terms your buyers search for. We also produce print-ready catalogues and trade show materials, because the industrial sector still closes deals in person at exhibitions across the Gulf.

## Turning online visibility into offline contracts

The goal is simple: when a procurement manager in Abu Dhabi, Riyadh, or Doha searches for your product category, your company should appear — with a website that gives them enough information to add you to their vendor shortlist. From there, your sales team takes over. Our clients in this sector typically see a 2-3x increase in inbound RFQ volume within six months of launching a rebuilt web presence and SEO programme, with the highest-quality leads coming from organic search rather than paid channels.
